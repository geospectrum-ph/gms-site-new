ABOUT US
WHO WE ARE


GEOSPECTRUM is a Filipino-owned business entity that caters to the growing need for accurate, up-to-date and timely information to support decision making and planning activities.

GEOSPECTRUM distributes high resolution satellite imagery and value-added products that address a wide range of applications.
