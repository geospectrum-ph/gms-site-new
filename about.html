ABOUT US
WHO WE ARE
GEOSPECTRUM is a Filipino-owned business entity that caters to the growing need for accurate, up-to-date and timely information to support decision making and planning activities.

GEOSPECTRUM distributes high resolution satellite imagery and value-added products that address a wide range of applications.
OUR TEAM  
01
VISION AND MISSION
WHAT WE WORK FOR

VISION. The leading people-centered developer and innovator of geospatial products, services, and analytics solutions in the Philippines.
MISSION. To enable actionable and data driven decisions by promoting high accuracy, reliable and affordable geospatial information enhanced by innovative analytics to support national development.
READ MORE  



VISION. To be the company of choice for leveraging geospatial data to generate actionable information and accurate, effective solutions.
MISSION. To harness our access to the continuous, real-time stream of geospatial datasets, state-of-the-art analytics tools, and the skills and competencies of our team to develop knowledge-based applications and meaningful solutions that are indispensable to decision making and planning.
