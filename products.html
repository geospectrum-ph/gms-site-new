WHAT WE OFFER


GEOSPECTRUM caters to the growing need for accurate, up-to-date and timely geospatial information to support decision making and planning activities. We distributes high resolution, high cadence (monthly, weekly and daily revisit) satellite imagery and value-added products that address a wide range of applications.

Satellogic’s hyperspectral cameras gather imagery data with a resolution of 25 meters while orbiting the Earth at an altitude of approximately 475 kilometers. The cameras capture data in 29 spectral bands ranging from visible to near-infrared wavelengths (460 - 830nm), and the resulting imagery is ortho-rectified and ready for use in Geographic Information Systems (GIS).

 
 
02.01
ICEYE SYNTHETIC APERTURE RADAR DATA
 
 
APPLICATIONS
  • Monitor any strategic site all the time (Port monitoring, maritime awareness)
  • Natural catastrophe monitoring (Oil spill monitoring)
  • Monitor forest in near real-time, and more

INDUSTRIES
  • Defense and Intelligence
  • Forestry
  • Civil Engineering and Architecture
  • Disaster Prevention
  • Fisheries
  • Government

 
ICEYE is revolutionizing synthetic aperture radar (SAR) technology with its small and agile SAR satellites, which are capable of revisiting the same location on Earth daily and even sub-daily. Unlike traditional SAR satellites, ICEYE's satellites are small and carry unique antennas that enable them to scan areas up to 50,000 km² in lower resolution and zoom into areas at a much higher resolution. With the world's largest SAR constellation, ICEYE provides unlimited global access and the highest frequency revisits on the market, measured in hours instead of days.

SEEDS FOR LGU

  • Formulation of economic development plans and land use policies

The Social Economic Environmental and Demographic Systems for LGU, or SEEDs for LGU, is a web GIS portal that provides Local Government Units (LGUs) a tool to effectively utilize spatial and textual information about and within their locality. It aims to provide a comprehensive visualization and analysis of data gathered from existing LGU data repositories, enabling them to query and analyze their locational information and assist them in providing efficient social services and enhancing development planning.
